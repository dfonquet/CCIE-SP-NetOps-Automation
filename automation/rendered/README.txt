Purpose:
  Stores rendered candidate configs before they are applied to devices.

What belongs here:
  Per-change and per-device IOS XR configuration files generated by the render
  workflow.

How it is used:
  Engineers review these files, CI publishes them as artifacts, and Ansible
  can use them for dry-run or deploy.

What you can add next:
  Add per-device diffs, approval notes, or links to CRQ records.

Note:
  Rendered configs are usually ignored by git. Regenerate them from YAML and
  Jinja2 templates.
